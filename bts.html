ABOUT

BTS(Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010 debuted under BigHit Official. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.BTS debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment with the single album 2 Cool 4 Skool

WORKS

BTS released their first single on June 12, 2013, and have been releasing songs and albums non-stop ever since then.
